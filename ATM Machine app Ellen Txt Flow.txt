ATM Machine app

Enter Pin

Check if pin valid

Ask what transaction they want

1. Display Amount  - print or view on screen

2. Deposit Amount
   Print out ticket

3. Withdraw Amount - check if enough cash to withdraw

   If yes - cash and print out ticket

If no - "insufficient funds"

   Would they like another crack at it

   Do check on enough cash to withdraw or insufficient funds

Last check - would they like another transaction  (go through loop).

No - print out ticket.
